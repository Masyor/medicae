Mission Statement

The Imperial Oversight Commission for the Regulation, Validation, and Re-Validation of Battlefield Medical Authorisation Forms, Tactical Augmentation Requests, and Priority Casualty Extraction Protocols (I.O.C.R.V.R.B.M.A.F.T.A.R.P.C.E.P.) is a heavily bureaucratised, Officio Medicae support office.

The main services we provide are: VIP target extraction, temporary battlefield augmentation, emergency medical treatment and paperwork.
